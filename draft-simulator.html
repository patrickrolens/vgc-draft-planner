Draft Simulator

Simulates a snake-style draft against the tier list. Each player is randomly assigned a drafting pattern — Top-heavy (splurge on top tiers, fill with scraps), Middle-heavy (spend evenly on mid tiers), Wildcard (grabs from a random tier every round) or Anchor (one marquee pick, then balanced) — plus a mega appetite of 1–2 (occasionally 3) and a type-synergy habit (strict drafters always take the best type fit at their chosen price, loose ones about half the time, off never) — avoiding stacked weaknesses and duplicate types and patching weaknesses they've already stacked. The odds of all three traits are adjustable under Drafter tendencies. Everyone always keeps enough budget back to fill their remaining slots. Megas are shown in yellow. Enter Your seat to take one of the chairs yourself and make that team's picks.
